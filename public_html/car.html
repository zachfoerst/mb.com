2024 Chevrolet Traverse vs. 2024 Hyundai Palisade: A Comprehensive Comparison

Overview

The 2024 Chevrolet Traverse and 2024 Hyundai Palisade are both popular three-row midsize SUVs designed for families. Let's compare these two vehicles across various aspects to help you make an informed decision.

Dimensions and Capacity

The Chevy Traverse is larger than the Hyundai Palisade:

Feature Chevy Traverse Hyundai Palisade
Length 204.7 inches 196.7 inches
Wheelbase 121 inches 114.2 inches
Seating Capacity Up to 8 Up to 8
Cargo Space (behind 3rd row) 22.9 cu ft 18.0 cu ft
Max Cargo Space 97.6 cu ft 86.4 cu ft

The Traverse offers more cargo space, making it a better choice for those who need extra storage capacity.

Performance

Both SUVs offer different powertrains:

Chevy Traverse:

  • Engine: 2.5L turbocharged 4-cylinder
  • Horsepower: 328 hp
  • Torque: 326 lb-ft
  • Drivetrain: AWD standard

Hyundai Palisade:

  • Engine: 3.8L V6
  • Horsepower: 291 hp
  • Torque: 262 lb-ft
  • Drivetrain: FWD or AWD available

The Traverse provides more power and torque, which may appeal to those seeking better performance.

Fuel Economy

The Traverse is slightly more fuel-efficient:

Model City Highway Combined
Chevy Traverse 21 mpg 28 mpg 23 mpg
Hyundai Palisade 19 mpg 26 mpg 22 mpg

These figures are for FWD models; AWD versions may have slightly lower fuel economy.

Interior and Features

Both SUVs offer well-appointed interiors with modern features:

  • The Palisade is known for its premium feel and upscale design.
  • The Traverse offers a spacious cabin with user-friendly technology.

Notable features:

  • Palisade: Available 12.3-inch digital gauge cluster
  • Traverse: Available 11-inch digital gauge cluster

Both vehicles come with advanced safety features and infotainment systems.

Pricing

The Hyundai Palisade has a slightly lower starting price:

  • Chevy Traverse: Starting from $38,995
  • Hyundai Palisade: Starting from $38,215

However, pricing can vary depending on trim levels and options.

Conclusion

The 2024 Chevrolet Traverse offers more power, space, and slightly better fuel economy. It's ideal for those who prioritize cargo capacity and performance. The 2024 Hyundai Palisade, while slightly smaller, provides a more premium feel and a lower starting price. Your choice will depend on your specific needs and preferences.
